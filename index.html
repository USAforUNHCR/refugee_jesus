JESUS WAS A REFUGEE

{% image UHW_logo.png class="uhw-logo" %}

Christmas Story

When Christ was a baby, his family was forced to flee their home to save his life. As the Bible tells us, Mary and Joseph made the difficult journey into Egypt, likely about 150 miles to escape threats to their lives and the life of their baby son from King Herod. Refugees in our time face the same threats as Joseph, Mary and the baby Jesus. They leave everything behind and undertake a perilous journey to search for safety and a chance for a better future.

Many times throughout the Bible, God uses seemingly weak and vulnerable people in His plans in powerful ways. The stories of these unlikely heroes illustrate that our beginnings do not have the power to determine our future. As we consider how to help those fleeing their homes, imagine Mary and Joseph on the night they fled to Egypt to save their little boy’s life.
